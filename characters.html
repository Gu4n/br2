Characters

Dela Delon

24-year-old sorceress and bounty hunter.

Convinced that Ares is related to the death of her old master Balcan 7 years earlier, she compulsively developed a sense to follow Ares wherever he goes (What really happened to Balcan remains unknown). While Dela's magical power is promising, her almost astonishingly short temper makes her prone to rash decisions and, more often than not, winds up in the traps she herself set for her arch-rival.

Ares

26-year-old wandering swordsman.

The bounty on Ares' head is worth 1.800.000 gold. He obtained the Planet Buster during his investigations of the buried nation of Vittoria. He originally worked as a mercenary, but the tasks that befell him often clashed with his belief to act according to his own judgement. Even when he was begged to help out others, he decided to turn all of them down. He became the subject of numerous tales of heroism, even though all of them are nothing more than sagas, indeed.

Karl Caless

26 years old.

Little is known about Karl's background until he and Ares became acquainted during their mercenary line of work. Following the oracle of prophet Benedicte, he was asked by King Badorrer of the lesser nation of Bundevia to take command of Ares' arrest. Hired as mercenary force leader, Karl faces a divide in his assignment and his feelings toward Ares in the form of comradery but also rivalry as swordsmen. Fighting on horseback is one of his fortes.
