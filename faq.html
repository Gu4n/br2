FAQ

Do I need to have played the first Brandish/The Dark Revenant to understand the story?

No, you can enjoy the game without having played its predecessor. We do recommend, however, that you play Brandish: The Dark Revenant first. The stories are intertwined. Having played the first game give you a better understanding and fuller appreciation of what is going on in the sequel. You will also enjoy numerous references to Ares' first exploration.

Wait, does Brandish even have a plot?

Sure does. Pretty deep world-building for an early 90s action RPG as well.
