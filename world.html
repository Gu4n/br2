World

Bundevia

Bundevia is remembered as the first state to be established by ancient overseas. They spread their civilisation from this place to every corner of the continent.

Bundevia's current ruler is the aging King Badorrer. The nation is situated to the northeast of Banourd, home of the series' protagonist Ares.

Bellsaddce

Rock islet, situated 4 kilometers of the Bundevian coast. It's approximately 100 hectares in size.

Its surface consists mostly of steep rock formation. The interior of the islet developed naturally into expansive limestone caves.

Vittoria

Ancient scriptures speak of Vittoria as the most developed state among the nations established by the ancients.

Once known for its successful authority over the source of omnipotence, now it is not even certain where the state was originally situated.
